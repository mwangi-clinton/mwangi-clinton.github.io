About Me

I’m a Master’s student in Telecommunication Engineering with a focus on applying Artificial Intelligence to healthcare. As a research intern at the Center for Data Science and AI (DsaIL) at Dedan Kimathi University of Technology, I develop AI-driven tools to assist orthopedic surgeons in diagnosing lower limb deformities.

My work blends computer vision, machine learning, and real-time data processing to enhance clinical decision-making and improve patient outcomes. I'm passionate about leveraging technology to advance early detection and personalized treatment in orthopedics.
